Our Coffees

Espresso

Espresso is a strong and concentrated coffee made by hot water through ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk foam.

Latte

A latte is made with 1 part coffee, 3 parts milk and then topped with foam.

Mocha

It is a combination of shot of espresso with steamed milk and chocolate and often comes topped with whipped cream.
